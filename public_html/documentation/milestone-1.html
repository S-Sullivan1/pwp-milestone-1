PWP Milestone One

Purpose, Audience & Goal

  1. Purpose: This project will be a portfolio website that displays personal talents of web development.
  2. Audience: This site is specifically designed for potential employers, with the primary audience being HR professionals and peers who share a similar educational and professional background.
  3. Goal: The primary objective of this website is to provide a platform for showcasing my current skill set, highlighting my achievements, and offering potential employers a direct means of reaching out to me.

Persona

Jeff Hardy

Age: 47

Profession:

Lead Hiring Manager at Sandi National Labs for the past 10 years. Looking for freshly trained fullstack web developers to build the website/database for an internal cataloging project. Specializes in selecting candidates who have a high chance of longevity with the company.

Technology

Utilizes an Android phone and an HP laptop running Windows 10.

Although not particularly skilled with computers, performs job-related tasks proficiently. Exclusively uses a phone and a computer, no tablet use.

Dispositions

Known at Sandi National Labs for relentless work ethic and unwavering commitment to finding the best talent, exhibits immense pride at hiring and approaches each recruitment process as a personal mission. Expect excellence from both self and the candidates evaluated. The biggest frustration is people not taking the process seriously. Known to be tough yet fair.

Goals

Determined to quickly judge and identify candidates aligned with the company's needs. While the objective is to locate an individual capable of creating and maintaining a website/database from the ground up. The person must also be able to work well within a team setting. The primary focus is to see if the person is an excellent fit for the company's future and long-term success.

User Story:

As a Lead Hiring Manager in this role, the task is to swiftly identify web developer candidates with good work ethics and team chemistry.
